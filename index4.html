ABOUT US

The College of Engineering Chengannur (CEC), was
established by the Government of Kerala under the
aegis of the IHRD, in the year 1993. It has been
approved by All India Council for Technical Education
(AICTE) and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological
University (KTU).Ever since its inception, the college has
maintained its eminence as a leading engineering
college in Kerala. Being located in Chengannur town,
Alleppey district the college has got access to all means
of transport, communication and lodging facilities.

CONTACT

CEC is located in Chengannur, a beautiful town in Kerala.Chengannur is situated at the extreme east part of Alappuzha district. Main Central Road (popularly known as the M.C. Road) passes by the college. It is well connected to other cities by rail and road.
